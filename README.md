## Biomedical Engineering Mission

I design and build systems at the intersection of **biomedical engineering**, **clinical signal intelligence**, and **embedded computing**.
My goal is to turn physiological data into robust, real-world decision support tools.

---

## Core Portfolio Tracks

| Track | Focus | Output |
|---|---|---|
| Clinical Signal Intelligence | ECG and physiological signal modeling, feature pipelines, model validation | Reproducible experiments and deployable prototypes |
| Biomedical AI Systems | Health-oriented AI layers, data workflows, clinical utility-first design | End-to-end research-to-application pipelines |
| Embedded + VLSI Engineering | Hardware-aware architecture, constrained compute, RTL and device integration | Prototypes for wearable and edge-health scenarios |
